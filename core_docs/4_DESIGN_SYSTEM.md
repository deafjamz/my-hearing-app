# Design System: "Aura"
**Role:** Product Designer (Accessibility Focus)

## Color Tokens (Tailwind)
* Background: `#0A0A0A` (OLED Black), `#1C1C1E` (Surface).
* Text: `#F2F2F7` (Primary), `#AEAEB2` (Secondary).
* Accents: `#00A79D` (Teal/Signal), `#FFB300` (Amber/Noise).

## Components
1.  **The Aura:** A visualizer using a radial gradient that pulses with audio amplitude.
    * Teal = Speech.
    * Amber = Noise.
2.  **Haptics:**
    * Success: Crisp "Bip-bip" (Transient).
    * Error: Heavy "Thud" (Continuous).
3.  **Accessibility:**
    * Minimum touch target: 54px.
    * Optical sizing for typography enabled.
By fusing cutting-edge audio technology with proven clinical methodologies, the SoundSteps "Active Comprehension" module provides a uniquely powerful and engaging platform for aural rehabilitation.
