# Brand Strategy: Accessible Premium
**Role:** CMO

## Visual Identity
* **Concept:** "The Apple of Hearing." Clean, Swiss, Medical-Grade.
* **No Cyberpunk:** Avoid neon/dystopian vibes.
* **Palette:** Deep Space Grey (`#1C1C1E`) + Bioluminescent Teal (`#00A79D`).
* **Typography:** Inter/SF Pro. High legibility.

## Growth Loops
1.  **The Spouse Loop:** A feature allowing a user's partner to record their voice via a web portal. The app then generates exercises using that specific voice.
2.  **The Prescription Pad:** A PDF resource for audiologists to "prescribe" the app without sales reps.
