**Concept:** Stepped S — uppercase S with flat truncated terminals, three horizontal staircase lines fanning outward at each terminal, deep teal #008F86 on dark #0A0E14

**Negative prompt:** not photorealistic, not 3d render, not mockup, not gradient background, not clipart
